Music

/resume Resume the paused playback. -
/skip Skip the current song. -
/stop Stop the playback and clear the queue. -
/leave Disconnect the bot from the voice channel. -
/queue Show the current song queue. -
/nowplaying Show information about the currently playing song. -
/shuffle Shuffle the song queue. -
/clear Clear the song queue. -
/remove Remove a song from the queue by its number. index
/volume Change the volume (0-200). level
/loop Set the loop mode. mode ('off', 'one', or 'all')
/join Connect the bot to your voice channel. -
/music_help Show the command list and usage for the music feature. -
/reload music_cog Reload the music cog to fix issues. -
